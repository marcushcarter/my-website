Now lets start makign our game. The first thing we should do is come up with a MVP (or minimum viable product) which is basically the simplest, smallest version of a product that does just enough to solve the core problem. SO what are the core concepts of flappy bird. Here's what I came up with:

  • character falls and flaps upwards when you tap
  • obstacles that move to the left
  • your score increases when you navigate past an obstacle
  • game ends if you touch an obstacle

So lets go one at a time through the different components. Let's start with the bird...
